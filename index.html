Projects

Intelligent Eye Disease Recognition Jan 2025 – May 2025
Problem: Multi-label classification of 8 eye diseases from retinal images to assist diagnosis.
Data: ODIR-5K dataset (5,000 fundus images).
Approach: Dual-image stitching + Gaussian blur; per-class Focal Loss; compared VGG19, ViT, ConvNeXt; ablation studies.
Outcome: ConvNeXt + per-class Focal Loss → AUC 0.951, F1 0.864 (minor-class F1 +10%).
My contribution (individual): Full pipeline design, loss implementation, all experiments.
🔗 Code (GitHub)  
CSIRO Pasture Biomass Prediction Sep 2025 – Feb 2026
Problem: Predict 5 biomass indicators from pasture images + auxiliary data for grazing management.
Data: CSIRO pasture dataset with NDVI, height, species, date.
Approach: ViT-Huge (DINOv3) dual-stream + FiLM fusion; two-stage training; GroupKFold CV, TTA, heuristic post-processing.
Outcome: Weighted R² = 0.71; biologically consistent predictions.
My contribution (group of 3): Project lead, dual-stream fusion design, two-stage training strategy, TTA implementation.
🔗 Code (GitHub)
Real-time Video Stream Monitoring Jan 2026 – present
Problem: Detect & track pedestrians/vehicles, identify abnormal events in urban streets.
Data: VIRAT dataset + custom street footage.
Approach: YOLOv8x + tracking, video stabilization, trajectory-based anomaly detection (speed, collision). Hourly flow & crowd analysis.
Outcome: Expected mAP >90%, anomaly detection rate >90%; full statistical reports.
My contribution (group of 4): System architecture, YOLOv8x integration, anomaly module, traffic/crowd analytics.
